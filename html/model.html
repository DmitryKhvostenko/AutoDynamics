Model description
KIA RIO X LINE is a reliable and versatile cross hatchback developed on the basis of of the same name fourth generation car. The car was created directly for the Russian market: the manufacturer will pay special attention to adapted suspension settings and increased road clearance up to 170 mm for the most comfortable ride not only on city roads, but also on off-road.
Progressive cross-hatchback in a new body is characterized by excellent dynamics and democratic cost, which will certainly appeal to young motorists who give preference for spectacular appearance and maximum practicality.
The front-wheel drive model is equipped with one of two petrol engines equipped with a 6-speed manual transmission or automatic transmission:
  • 100 hp 1.4 liter Kappa engine;
  • 1.6-liter Gamma power unit with 123 hp.
The spectacular exterior of the KIA Rio X-Line is decorated:
  • lensed head optics;
  • radiator grille with chrome finish and elements of black gloss;
  • round fog lights;
  • roof rails;
  • exclusive 16” alloy wheels.
In a spacious ergonomic cabin, the driver and passengers feel as comfortable as possible thanks to such interior elements as:
  • comfortable heated seats with eco-leather upholstery;
  • folding rear seats;
  • multifunctional heated steering wheel, adjustable in height and on departure;
  • modern multimedia system with 7" color monitor Touch Screen and navigator.
