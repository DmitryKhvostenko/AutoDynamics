Insurance in Auto Dynamics

By purchasing a car in "Auto Dynamics" you can minimize risks associated with its ownership and operation, because the car is a means of increased danger. The best way is to insure your car.


We will offer the best conditions from leading insurance companies at individual rates, developed taking into account the maximum possible discounts, as well as a number of additional services.

We provide the following insurance services:
ALI
ALI (Automobile Liability Insurance) is a type of auto insurance that provides coverage for damages and injuries caused to others in the event of an accident. It is a mandatory insurance requirement in many states in the United States. ALI protects drivers from financial liability by compensating third parties for medical expenses, property damage, and other losses resulting from the insured driver's negligence or fault.
CAI
CAI (Comprehensive Auto Insurance) is a type of car insurance coverage that provides protection against a wide range of risks and damages, excluding collisions. It covers losses caused by theft, vandalism, natural disasters, falling objects, fire, and other non-collision incidents. Comprehensive auto insurance helps to repair or replace your vehicle if it is damaged or lost due to covered events. It offers peace of mind by providing financial protection beyond just accidents and collisions.
